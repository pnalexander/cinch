Typography

Typography with Cinch is optimized for you and your users to admire. The Oxygen typeface distributed by Google is Cinch's type base and we think you'll love it. There are also several utility classes built-in to take your coding to the next level of simplicity.

Forms

Forms are made simple with Cinch. No more pulling your hair out to get things right. Cinch utilizes built-in HTML5 form validation and will soon have the option to handle forms with a handy API that does all the hard work for you.
